Who We Are

A Space Where You Never Have to Pretend You Know Wine

Purple Corkscrew is a boutique wine shop and tasting room in the heart of Avondale Estates. Founded by Steffini Bethea in 2012, we were built on a simple idea: wine should be welcoming, never stuffy.

Our team tastes roughly 50 wines a week to curate a collection that has something for everyone — from bold reds and crisp whites to exciting discoveries from around the world.

Our Story

Born from a Trip to Spain

Before wine, Steffini was in pharmaceutical sales. After being laid off in 2010, a trip to Spain introduced her to wines she'd never experienced in the U.S. — and she couldn't stop exploring. Her husband told her: "If you're going to drink wine like this, you need to figure out a way to make money doing it."

Purple Corkscrew was born. First in Emory Village, then finding its true home in Avondale Estates — a close-knit, walkable community that matched the shop's warmth perfectly. Thirteen years later, the staff is family, the regulars are friends, and every bottle on the shelf has been personally vetted.

Steffini Bethea
Founder & Owner
